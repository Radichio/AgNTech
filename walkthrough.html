02

The investor package

This is the tool you described — a package that does the diligence automatically (market, comparables, a claims-check, your network) and hands you a finished memo to review and judge. Here's everything it includes, and a sample of what comes out the other end.

What every package includes

  1. The thesis — why this, why now your call
  2. The opportunity — what they do, stage, traction auto your read
  3. Market — size, growth, timing, with sources auto
  4. Comparables — what similar rounds priced at auto
  5. Claims-check — the deck vs the verifiable facts auto
  6. Strengths & risks auto your read
  7. The structure — how the deal & capital come together your call
  8. The ask — what's needed, on what terms your call
  9. Why AgNtech is in it — your stake & value-add your call
A sample of what gets produced — for one deal
Tilth Labs — Series A
Agtech · variable-rate application · prepared by AgNtech Connect
The thesis

Best traction in the current pipeline, in a category being re-rated, with two operators we trust already paying. The window favours moving now rather than in six months.

The opportunity

Variable-rate application platform for row crops. $640k ARR, growing ~18% month over month; two portfolio peers already customers. Capital-efficient to date — real product, real users.

Market

Variable-rate software adoption up ~22% year over year; category leaders being marked up as precision-ag budgets shift to software. [sources cited in the data room]

Comparables

Comparable Series A rounds priced $25–40M post on similar ARR and growth.

Claims-check

Company figures reconcile with independent diligence; no material discrepancies. (When they don't, this section flags it — e.g. "deck claims $180k ARR; listed pilots sum to ~$120k.")

Strengths & risks

Strengths: strongest traction in the batch; warm references from operators we know; capital-efficient.  Risks: valuation expectations may run ahead of the stage.

The structure

Lead a $3M round; syndicate the balance through a growth-stage agtech fund in the network. AgNtech takes a principal stake.

The ask

$3M Series A; target close in 60 days. References and full data room on request.

Why AgNtech is in it

Investing alongside, with operator help on channel and go-to-market — and the relationships for the capital this company needs next. We don't introduce and walk away.

Assembled by the tool, approved by Terry before it goes out. Figures sourced and current as of the date of issue. For discussion among qualified parties; not an offer of securities or investment advice.
