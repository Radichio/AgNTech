Careers

The team we’re building.

AgNtech Connect is early, and deliberately small. As the work grows — more capital, more ventures, more building — so will the people behind it. This is where that team will take shape.

We’re not actively hiring today. But we’d rather meet good people early than scramble later. If the work below is the kind you want to do, introduce yourself — we keep every serious note on file.
Where we’re headed

Three kinds of people we’ll build around.

The work sits at the meeting point of agriculture, technology, and capital — so the team will too.

Technology & engineering

Software engineers, AI and data people, and hardware builders — the ones who turn an agtech concept into something real, and who help the ventures we back move fast.

Advisory & analysis

Analysts and associates who can read a deal, pressure-test a market, and build the packages that put an opportunity in front of serious capital.

Operations & partnerships

People who keep the engine running and the relationships warm — coordinating deals, managing the network, and making the client experience feel effortless.

For international builders

Talent belongs wherever it’s found.

Some of the best technologists, engineers, and operators for this work will come from outside Canada. As we grow, we intend to build a team that welcomes skilled newcomers and immigrants who want to help build the future of Canadian agriculture. Our approach to relocation and work authorization will be spelled out as specific roles open — if that’s you, reach out early and we’ll be straight with you about where things stand.
