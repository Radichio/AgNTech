About

A farmer who learned to move capital.

I'm Terry Cholka. For forty years I've built things that grow — a farm, companies, and the relationships that let serious capital reach Canadian agriculture. AgNtech Connect is where that work lives now.

The road here

I broke into farming in the 1980s with more nerve than money. I came up through the years that finished other operators — the BSE crisis among them — and kept going. That's where I learned what's real and what isn't, and how to tell the difference quickly.

I grew a farm from 480 acres to more than 10,000, and built it into multiple enterprises. Alongside it, I helped other companies — from start-up to exits — as an investor, director, or advisor: across agriculture, technology, and bioscience. I was an early backer of a Canadian hemp-foods company acquired for more than $400 million. I financed and delivered a feature film. Different worlds, one instinct.

I can turn a concept into reality.

None of it came from a fund or a title. It came from being on the ground — knowing the operators, the regulators, and the difference between a deck and a business. That's the judgment I bring to every introduction now.

Why this work

I do this because the rural economy is worth more than it's been allowed to become. There is real capital looking for a way into Canadian agriculture, and real builders who need more than a cheque — and most of them never find each other. Closing that gap, on terms that hold, is work worth doing.

The aim is bigger than any single deal, and I work with people who share it — capital and founders alike.

The aim

To bring people and resources together, from wherever they are, to build the systems behind sustainable agriculture, secure food, and a healthy environment — and to help the rural economy reach its full, sustainable potential, rooted here in Manitoba.

How AgNtech Connect works

AgNtech Connect is a navigator, not a fund. The work is finding the opportunities only a local operator can reach, vetting them hard, structuring them cleanly, and helping open the way to the capital they need — then staying in afterward. Three things run alongside each other:

Bring capital in

Connecting governments, funds, and family offices to vetted opportunities in Canadian agriculture and technology.

Build companies

Backing founders as an investor, director, and advisor — from start-up through to exit.

Educate & advise

Putting four decades on the land and in the boardroom to work for the people who ask.

And the interests stay aligned. I take a stake in the outcome — my read is only worth something if it proves right.
